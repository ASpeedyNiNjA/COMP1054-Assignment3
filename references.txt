Cadbury has been using the purple color since 1914, when it was introduced as a tribute to Queen Victoria.
The official colour of Cadbury Purple / Violet is Spanish Violet (#482683). 
The Cadbury Purple/Violet logo colour represents luxury. 
The color scheme can be used for design projects and purposes. 
The company was given a royal warrant in 1854, making it the official cocoa and chocolate makers for the British monarch.
