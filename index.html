Cadbury Caramilk

What is the Caramilk secret?

Cadbury has been using the purple color since 1914, when it was introduced as a tribute to Queen Victoria. The official colour of Cadbury Purple / Violet is Spanish Violet (#482683). The Cadbury Purple/Violet logo colour represents luxury. The color scheme can be used for design projects and purposes. The company was given a royal warrant in 1854, making it the official cocoa and chocolate makers for the British monarch.

Cadbury Curly Wurly

Tastes like this feels

Curly Wurly is a brand of chocolate bar manufactured by Cadbury and sold worldwide. It was launched in the UK in 1970. Its shape resembles three flattened, intertwined serpentine strings. The bar is made of chocolate-coated caramel.
